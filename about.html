A little about us...

The vision behind Coco's initially came into fruition in 1995 when our founders opened our 1st and only restaraunt in downtown Vancouver. Lovers of all things Mexican, especially their highly diverse and flavourful foods, we recruited the best Mexican chef in the province.

Our Vision:

More than 2 decades later, Coco's is livlier than ever with it's passion for serving excellent food and it's hospitality. We hope to continue with our dedication towards serving the best Mexican food in town, be it our Carnitas Tacos or our delicios Hapa Burritos.

Our commitment:

Coco's is committed to using only the healthiest and most organic ingredients sourced locally from BC! We also cater to both Non-Vegetarian and Vegan customers.
